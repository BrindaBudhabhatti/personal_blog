About Me

My Story:

As a kid, I was always taking things apart to see how they worked. This curiosity naturally led me to computers
and tinkering with them. Learning about how to code was like unlocking a secret language for these machines,
and it opened up a whole new world of possibilities for me. When I discovered AI and ML, it was like stepping
into the future! These fields are constantly evolving, and there's so much potential to create new things
and solve problems that we can't even imagine yet.



My Approach:

I believe that anyone can learn to code and explore technology. It's all about
asking questions, trying new things, and not giving up when things get tough. I
also think it's important to have fun while you're learning!
